Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the "Immortal Inscriptions" baseball set?

The "Immortal Inscriptions" set from the 2005 Upper Deck Ultimate Signature Edition is a prestigious collection of on-card autographs from baseball's greatest legends. What makes this set unique is that the players included specific inscriptions, often their famous nicknames (like "The Wiz" for Ozzie Smith or "Lefty" for Steve Carlton) or Hall of Fame induction years, adding a personal and historical touch to each card.

Who are the key players in this 2005 Ultimate Signature collection?

This collection features several Hall of Fame inductees and baseball icons, including Ozzie Smith, Jim Bunning, and Steve Carlton. It also includes modern legends like Eric Gagne, known for his "Game Over" inscription. The cards are all limited editions, with serial numbers typically ranging from /75 to /99, ensuring their rarity in the marketplace.

What is a "Buy Back Autograph" in baseball card collecting?

A "Buy Back Autograph" occurs when a card manufacturer (like Upper Deck) re-purchases original cards from previous years, has the player sign them, and then re-inserts them into a new product. This collection features a 2001 SP Buy Back Autograph of Ken Griffey Jr., one of the most sought-after icons in the history of the sport.

Are these vintage and modern baseball cards available for sale?

These cards are part of a private curated collection and are not currently available for sale. The gallery is intended for educational and appreciation purposes for fellow baseball card collectors and sports historians.
