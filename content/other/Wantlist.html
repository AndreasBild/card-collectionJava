Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What makes the Juwan Howard "Wantlist" so special?

This wantlist represents the "final pieces of the puzzle" for my extensive Juwan Howard player collection. It specifically targets ultra-rare 90s parallels, high-end 1/1 masterpieces, and short-printed inserts like the 1998-99 Upper Deck Black Diamond Quadruple (#/150) and the elusive SPx Finite Extreme (#/1). These cards are the holy grails for any serious Juwan Howard collector.

Which Juwan Howard cards are currently your top priority?

My absolute top priorities are the 1998-99 Upper Deck Black Diamond Quadruple and any 1999-00 1/1 masterpieces from brands like SkyBox (APEX, Hoops) or Upper Deck (SPx, Hardcourt). I am also actively seeking the 2003-04 Topps Finest X-Fractor 1/1. If you have any of these or other rare Juwan Howard parallels, please get in touch!

Are you looking to buy or trade for these specific basketball cards?

Yes! I am actively looking to acquire these cards to complete my collection. I am open to both direct purchases and potential trades depending on your interests. My goal is to preserve these pieces of basketball history in a dedicated player collection.

How can I reach out if I have a card on your wantlist?

I am always excited to connect with fellow collectors who might have leads on these rare items. You can reach me directly via email at seraph@gmx.co.uk. Whether you have a card for sale or just want to talk Juwan Howard memorabilia, I'd love to hear from you!
