What is this wantlist about?

This list Wantlist contains the few missing Juwan Howard cards in my private collection. My most sought after cards are the 1998-98 Upper Deck Black Diamond Quadruple and the 1998-99 SPX Finite Extreme

Are any of these cards for sale?

Currently, the items in this collection are not for sale. This website serves as a gallery to share my collection with fellow enthusiasts and to keep track of what I have.
