Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is this collection about?

This collection is a private assortment of the Panini Flawless Basketball set from 2012-13, focusing on specific players and autographs. This was one of the biggest Autograph sets and i like the design of the cards.

Are any of these cards for sale?

Currently, the items in this collection are not for sale. This website serves as a gallery to share my collection with fellow enthusiasts and to keep track of what I have.

How can I contact you about your collection?

I'm always open to discussing sports cards with other collectors! You can reach out to me via the contact information provided on the main page of this site.
